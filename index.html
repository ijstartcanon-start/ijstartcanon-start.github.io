ij.start.canon



Ij.start.canon is all about downloading and installing the driver or software for your device. The driver is the link between the device and the canon Ij printer. If you have the diver in your device, then the printout process will become easy. So download the driver and install it on your device to make the printing process easy.


Set Up Your Canon Inkjet Printer


  • Unbox and Assemble: Carefully unbox your printer or scanner. Follow the included instructions to assemble any removable parts.
  • Power On: Plug in your device and turn it on. Make sure it’s connected to a power source.
  • Connect to Wi-Fi: Most Canon printers and scanners support wireless connectivity. Use the control panel to connect to your Wi-Fi network.
  • Install Drivers: Visit the Canon support site to download and install the necessary drivers for your specific model. You can find these drivers at ij.start.canon.
  • Test Print/Scan: Print a test page or scan a document to ensure everything is working correctly.

Easy steps to download the Canon Printer Driver


  • Open your web browser and go to ij.start.cannon.
  • Now, you will be asked to choose your preferred language and your country.
  • After that, you will be redirected to the sign up page.
  • Login to your account by providing the required login details.
  • Create an account if you are a new user.
  • Then, you will be taken to the Product selection window.
  • Enter the model number of your printer and then, click “Go”.
  • Select your device and you will then see a download button.
  • Click the download button to start the downloading process.

Prerequisite before you install Canon Printer Drivers


  • Keep your model number updated or reinstall it.
  • Make sure your printer is properly plugged in.
  • Connect the device with an internet connection.
  • Update the operating driver from settings or online.
  • Check whether your system has enough space or not.

Install Canon Printer Drivers & connect your Printer


  • Initiate with turning on your canon printer and operating system.
  • Connect the printer to your device.
  • After that, run the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions.
  • The wizard will now ask you to choose your connection type. Choose the desired alternative.
  • Finally, you can complete the drivers and software installation.

How to connect the canon printer Manually with your device?


  • Firstly, connect the one end of the USB cable to the canon Ij printer.
  • Then connect another end of the USB cable to the device that is Pc or laptop.
  • Then turn on the printer.
  • After that, turn on the device too.
  • Then in the start option of Pc or laptop.
  • Then in the start option, search for the printer.
  • The printer and Scanners option will appear; click on it.
  • Then printer and scanners will open.
  • There you will see Add device option; click on it.
  • Then you will see your device in the list.
  • Click on Add device option.
  • That's it, your canon Ij printer is added to your Pc or laptop.
